# The Fortnight Audit

A free tool from [lifestack](https://lifestack.studio): a two-week time audit for business owners, read across the eight domains of your life, with a branded report you can download as a PDF.

**Use it here: https://tools.lifestack.studio** — copy the prompt, paste it into your AI assistant (Claude recommended; ChatGPT, Gemini and Copilot also work), and follow along. The audit runs entirely inside your own AI session: lifestack never sees your calendar or your results.

This repository contains the published site (static, self-contained) and its Docker deployment.
