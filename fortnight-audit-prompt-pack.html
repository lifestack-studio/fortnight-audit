The Fortnight Audit

A two-week audit of where your time actually goes — read, honestly, across the eight domains of your life.

What this is

Ten minutes now for a fortnight of clarity

If you run a business, your calendar is the truth. This free tool walks you through a two-week time audit — the first lap of the lifestack method — then hands you a clean, branded report showing exactly where your hours went, split into strategic and operational work.

Then it goes one honest step further: a whole-life reading of what the same fortnight says across the eight domains of the lifestack — vocation, health, relationships, finances, growth, purpose, recreation, identity — with three concrete moves and a clear place to begin.

It's calm, honest, and quick. No sign-up, no data collection.

Privacy: the audit runs entirely inside your own AI session. lifestack never sees your calendar or your results.

How to use it

Four steps

Recommended: Claude (with Artifacts). Also works with ChatGPT, Gemini & Copilot.
  1. Open your AI assistant. We recommend Claude — it renders your report as a page you can download.
  2. Press Copy prompt below and paste it into a new chat.
  3. Follow along — upload a calendar screenshot or just describe your fortnight, confirm the categories it suggests, then answer five short reflection questions (any of them skippable).
  4. Get your branded report and download it as a PDF.
